 * RADIOHEAD - Permanent Daylight
 * My Iron Lung EP
 * Guitar tab by Stewart Peters <martyp@alphalink.com.au>

Works best in Open G tuning: DBGDGD, from 1st (high) string to 6th (low)
string. All re-tuned strings are tuned down.  Although there is little
or no use of the 1st & 6th strings I would still recommend tuning them
down to  D.  Note that the time signature changes from 4/4 to 6/8. The
strumming pattern is real easy though.
