["The Vault" contains live performances 'from the vault' that can be programmed daily,
 Monday through Friday, at the same time for a 'live at 5:00' type feature - all tracks
 feature both an announcer and commercials, but are also included 'clean' (no voice-overs,
 no commercials) at the end of the disc]
