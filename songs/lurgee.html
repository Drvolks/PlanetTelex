   Written at the same time as Creep, this is one of the band's favourite songs off Pablo Honey, and is played frequently live today. It's about losing someone who's been fucking you up for ages, and feeling much better for it. It's pronounced 'L-er-gh-ee', and is an English slang name for a nasty infectious disease.
