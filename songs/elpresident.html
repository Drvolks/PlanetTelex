   A duet between Thom Yorke and Isobelle Monteiro (great friends) of the band Drugstore. Recorded in 1996 but unreleased until Drugstore's new album came out in 1998, due to legal difficulties with their record company. There is a video for this single, and as part of an April's Fool joke, Thom and Isobelle spread the rumour that they appeared naked and bound with thick ropes. Which, of course, they don't.
