   Not written by the band - this is a cover of a song by Carly Simon. She famously sang it as the theme tune to the James Bond film : 'The Spy Who Loved Me'. Thom thinks it is the "sexiest song that was ever written" (afterwards, at the KROQ concert, the band played Creep, which he called the "second sexiest song ever written"). The band love it, as it is a complete antithesis to the sometimes depressing and downbeat music they normally play - a great pop tune. Played very occasionally as a live encore.
