   Unreleased as yet, the band have occasionally performed this song prior to the release of OK Computer. May be called 'Mortigi Tempo' or 'Awaits'.

   Recently, the band have been soundchecking with this song, building up to the new album. Phil has said this will "definitely be on the new album". People at the recent Tibetan Freedom Concert could hear the band playing about with the first few bars of the song during the soundcheck. (Listen to the first few minutes of our recording).
