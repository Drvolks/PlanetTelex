   A B-side recorded in the UK in November 1997 quite clearly inspired by all the Airbag/DJ Shadow stuff. Palo Alto, California is in Silicon Valley, and has the motto "city of the future". It is home to many large corporations such as Xerox.

   The band have never worked out how to play it live properly. This song was originally known as 'OK Computer' but was ditched pretty early on in the recording of that LP, to be revived and overhauled for release as a B-side.
