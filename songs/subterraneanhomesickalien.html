   An old song which was just being written as The Bends was released in March 1995, and performed many times since. The band were trying to achieve the effect of Miles Davies' 'Bitches Brew' with a sub-aqua sound of electric keyboards and soft guitars. The song used to be called 'Uptight'. When played live, Thom plays the Rhodes piano.

It was recorded in their Oxford studios, Canned Applause.
