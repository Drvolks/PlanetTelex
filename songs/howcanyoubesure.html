   This song appeared in the soundtrack to the film 'Nowhere'. In Japan, it goes under the name 'When I'm Like This'. One of Colin's favourites, he really wanted it to be included on The Bends, but he didn't get his way. This song was first written and demoed back in early 1993.

   The backing vocals are listed on the CD as being done by 'Dianne Swann'. However, I believe this is an in-joke (Dianne Swann = 'dying swan', a slang phrase for someone making an awful noise), and the person is really Ed (or possibly Thom), just engineered well. When it is played live, Ed sings the backing vocals.
