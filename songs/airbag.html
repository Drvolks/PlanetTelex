    Originally entitled 'An Airbag Saved My Life', this song is a tongue-in-cheek tribute to Indeep's 1983 disco hit 'Last Night a DJ Saved My Life'. They were inspired by DJ Shadow to use cut-up drum beats. The song is very complex and layered, and many parts had to carefully mixed together to achieve the end result.

    The song was originally performed by the band acoustically back in 1995. According to Thom, the song itself is about the feeling you get when you realise that you've just missed having a serious accident, and the feeling of elation that follows. It was inspired by Thom's feeling towards cars (Stupid Car, Killer Cars...) after being involved in a car crash with his girlfriend when he was younger. The song is one of Thom's favourites off OK Computer. The title 'An Airbag Saved My Life' was a headline that Thom read in an AA ('Automobile Association', British car roadside recovery company) magazine that came through his door.
