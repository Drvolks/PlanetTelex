Shhhh! "Stir Fry Noodles!" "Crispy Duck" "Free Tibet" "Sweet And Sour Veg" ", yeah, we started Chinese Whispers that there was a pop explosion, around 1995. It's called Pop 2000 cos after that, hopefully thinking can change.

Trainspotting? Hardly a description of thinking OUTSIDE the accepted TRAIN of thought. Self opinion destined to glorious failure but being so mad for music, we're so sad that we can t get the smile off our faces. (Quick, Nurse...the dictionary! Look up "sad").

Well, last year the words "Pop Explosion" appeared in the national press. Last week it was all over for British Music. This week everything's great for British Music. They are both right but what if there really was not just a third way, but as many ways as thinking would allow. "Brit ___", "Cool Brit ___", "Britthis Britthat", Nothing to do with us. A different way of thinking. Wales, Eire, Scotland, Ulster, England, Channel Islands - these small clumps of land on the planet are THE ROCK AND ROLL ISLES!

You can think human, territorial, them and us. Or you can think alien, love, peace, friendship, kindness, beauty, UNITY

Take a head trip. Ever sat on a bus, in a train, on a plane, with lots of other people all minding their own business. There could be a "book" written about every person in that space. What's happening inside their head is plain speaking. Nowadays plain speaking is as much of an art form as a plain speaking movie like Mike Leigh.

Sitting next to the Mike Leigh movie inside one head, could be Carry On Films, Catherine Cookson, Spike Lee, Oscar Wilde, John Lennon, Doris Day, Trainspotting, Martin Luther King, The Beastie Boys or Rudolf Hess.

Brains as far apart as the planets in the universe. Millions of miles and stars millions of light years away are all inside our individually unique minds. A galaxy inside every head. The planet that we live on providing everything that we need, both natural and unnatural (PLASTIC IS NOT FROM MARS).

The planet that we live on is more complicated in nature than any computer. We are made in it's image (complicated and mostly composed of water) and of it's life forms a species of the animal kingdom that self culls not out of necessity

Plants, Trees, Animals, Fishes, Insects, Birds, Reptiles, Flora - The aliens might be interested in the lifeforms of this planet or not - let's not take things for granted. They may notice that the big tall brown ones with the green tops are what the smaller moving noisy ones need to live. They may notice that the smaller moving noisy ones use machinery and come in all shades mixed from a palette of cream, red and brown and that they destroy the planet that provides everything from them, chop down the tall still ones and eat the other moving noisy ones that don t use machinery and come in a greater variety of shades (but don't notice or group according to shade like the other small noisy ones).

And each of the small noisy ones that come in less shades and use machinery is distinctly unique. Each individual's politics, tastes, beliefs and life story is distinctly unique. The one thing that they all have in common is that they all have this distinct uniqueness in common - a cause of celebration and UNITY.

BELIEF IS AS MUCH IN THE MIND OF THE BELIEVER AS BEAUTY IS IN THE EYE OF THE BEHOLDER SO ALL RELIGIONS, LIKE ALL PEOPLE, ARE EQUAL.

Every religion is for love, peace and tolerance, but sometimes tolerance draws the line at other religions. What if it wasn't that way. What if THE WAY THINGS ARE could be another way. What if the best food distributors to the world were more acclaimed businesses than arms dealers. What if nurses, firemen and teachers were on equal par to captains of industry? What if people became more important than money? What if money, position, power and all that silly bollocks were worth nothing compared to having your self belief or your belief in others rewarded?

What if people looked at money and saw it. Saw that it was pieces of metal and paper and that they couldn't eat it, drink it, wear it, heal themselves with it or shelter under it so they should spread it evenly across the people because every (equal, remember) person needs to eat, drink, clothe, heal and house themselves as much as the paper/metal freaks.

The eccentricity of the small sporting islands above the Europe land mass are a good breeding ground for the arts. A universe of minds steeped in this unique culture means that per capita, the Rock N Roll Isles must have highest concentration of arts and the industries and media around them. What if the lateral thinking that could allow all religions to be equally may prefer THE ARTS TRADE instead of THE ARMS TRADE?

From this train of thought comes the soundtrack of the 90s. It's not a 60's revival, just "60" upside down. The lyrics do mean something and even if they don't the tunes do. This soundtrack could be as important to you as sport is allowed to be. A history steeped in art and sport is as important as that sparkly hat in The Tower Of London. The future could be nurtured with a positive culture. The soundtrack of the 90s has no boundaries, it's rap of lyrics is an education, it's tunes beautifully evocative.

Hence the fax "FEEL FREE TO DISAGREE BUT THERE IS ALWAYS MORE THAN ONE WAY OF LOOKING AT THINGS".
