   Thom : "It was written around the time of My Iron Lung and was one of those songs that completely wrote itself. It was all coming out of this one riff. I love stuff like Stereolab where they repeat riffs over and over again."

   For the video, Jonathan Glazer (Karmacoma, Karma Police videos) had been bugging the band to let him produce the video if this song ever became a single. They did with spectacular results.

   Until Paranoid Android, this was the band's best-placed chart entry, and is still a very popular song. Are the band getting slightly tired of playing it however?
