   Originally appearing on their very first demo tape, this song came out as a single in the USA where it was mixed by Chris Sheldon into a different version which has strings in and without the screaming in the middle. The band made a video for this single in which they were persuaded to "dress up in diver's clothes and dance around. It was all very embarrassing" (Thom). As far as he can tell, this was a "single that never was a single". Written as a tribute to The Pixies. Although it sounds nothing like them...
