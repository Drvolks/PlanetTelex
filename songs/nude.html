    A new acoustic song (just Thom) was premiered at the Tokyo show on 23rd January 1998, and has since been played on the US tour, with the rest of the band - a slow tempo song with Thom on Rhodes piano (as on Subterranean Homesick Alien), with Ed on guitar, and Jonny on Hammond and Glockenspiel. Towards the end of the song, Jonny plays both instruments - one with each hand! Despite Thom claiming it had only been written "4 days ago" when played in Dallas on 29th March, it has the same lyrics as the acoustic one previewed in Tokyo (which he called "unreleased").

   Although claimed to be new, the song is actually a reworking of the old song 'Nude' (also misspelt as 'Neut'), written as early as 1996. See the official pages for Thom's comments on it.

   Although initially invented on the Internet, the title 'Big Ideas (Don't Get Any)' which Thom heard for the first time from Matt Pinfield on MTV's 120 Minutes has actually stuck, and the official title is currently '(Don't Get Any) Big Ideas (Cause They're Not Going to Happen)'. He wanted to call it 'Your home is at risk if you do not keep up with payments' - after what comes up at the bottom of mortgage commercials, but he felt it wasn't catchy enough.
