   This is one of the songs which the band hates the most. It has only been played a very few times live by the band, grudgingly in 1995. Originally written about the Hungerford Massacre in England, hence Thom feels uncomfortable about it.

   Thom : "This was the last song we did for The Bends. There's thousands of tambourines on it, but you can't really hear it on the final mix. It was a weird song because it went from being the one that the record company insisted was going to be the hit to the least-liked thing on the album."
