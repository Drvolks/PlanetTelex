    The song existed in an acoustic version for a long time and was occasionally played live before it was recorded for the 'Axe the Act!' compilation (Thom: "It's going to do more good on there than on the (second) LP") and then later included on the Street Spirit single with full guitar. The band still play it today, dedicating it to the road crew.
