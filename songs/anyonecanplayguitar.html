   Radiohead's second single and first top 40 chart hit in the UK. The video was shot in a disused swimming pool in Hornsey, London. Nowadays, Thom sings "I want to be in this band when I get to heaven". Reached 32 in the charts, yet denied any radio play by Radio One in England because "we were singing about playing guitars and stuff".
