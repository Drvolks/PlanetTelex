Information for Subscribing
The Radiohead Mailing List is dedicated to communication between Radiohead fans. The posts (to the regular list) are unmoderated and threads range to almost all topics.

Radiohead-Announce:

The announce list is moderated and contains only new Radiohead news. This list sends messages about once or twice a week with news/setlists and band info. This list is recommended for those that can't take the heavy traffic of the RHML. All posts to this list are also sent to the RHML so there's no need to subscribe to both.

Radiohead Mailing List (Regular):

This list resends all contents to your mailbox, so when someone posts, the post gets forwarded to your mailbox. The list generally gets from 50-200 posts a day so your mailbox will be flooded with content, we recommend you use your e-mail client to filter RHML material to a seperate folder. This list is nice because you can see all topics (to keep up with threads) and receive messages immediatly.

Radiohead Mailing List (Digest):

The Digest is a compiled version of the regular list. All the posts from the day get sent out in one big mail (therefore much less traffic).

Posting:

All posts for the regular list are sent to radiohead@lists.underworld.net and information to go on the radiohead-announce list should be sent to rhnews@underworld.net. Thank you.
