   Radiohead's third major single "dedicated to the press, as always" (Thom). The words to the last chorus change as Thom feels like it. Written by Thom just after hearing that their second release of Creep had hit the Top 10. Played quite frequently during their earlier shows, but it never made it onto an album. The band don't like the song at all and Thom has admitted that "it's just a bad song". The last performance of this song was at the London Astoria gig of 1994. They don't play it any more.
