    A new acoustic song (just Thom) was premiered at the Tokyo show on 23rd January 1998, and has since been played on the US tour, with the rest of the band - a slow tempo song with Thom on Rhodes piano (as on Subterranean Homesick Alien), with Ed on guitar, and Jonny on Hammond and Glockenspiel. Towards the end of the song, Jonny plays both instruments - one with each hand! Despite Thom claiming it had only been written "4 days ago" when played in Dallas on 29th March, it has the same lyrics as the acoustic one previewed in Tokyo.

   Although claimed to be new, the song is actually a reworking of the old song 'Nude' (also known as 'Neut'). See the official pages for Thom's comments on it.
