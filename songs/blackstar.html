   Another song which has personal significance to Thom, and is possibly to a girlfriend of his. It is about "Sex in the morning. Which is the best time to have it. As long as you've cleaned your teeth beforehand". It is the band's favourite off The Bends, and it is occasionally played live today.

   The band Drugstore covered Black Star during a few of their live shows. They altered it a bit, using strings instead of guitars and slowing it down, making it more of a ballad.
